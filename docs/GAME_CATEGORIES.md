# Game Categories

This document organizes the 22 available games by category to help you find the type of game you're looking for.

## Classic Board Games

Traditional board games that have been played for centuries.

### Chess ♟️

- **Complexity**: High
- **Strategy Depth**: Very High
- **Players**: 2
- **Average Game Length**: 40-60 moves
- **Key Features**: Full rule implementation, PGN support

### Checkers 🔴⚫

- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Strategy Depth**: Medium
- **Players**: 2
- **Average Game Length**: 40-70 moves
- **Key Features**: Forced jumps, king promotion

### Go 🔵⚪

- **Complexity**: Very High
- **Strategy Depth**: Extreme
- **Players**: 2
- **Average Game Length**: 150-300 moves
- **Key Features**: Territory control, capture mechanics, SGF support

### Reversi (Othello) ⚫⚪

- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Strategy Depth**: High
- **Players**: 2
- **Average Game Length**: 60 moves
- **Key Features**: Flipping mechanics, corner strategy

### Mancala 🌰

- **Complexity**: Low-Medium
- **Strategy Depth**: Medium
- **Players**: 2
- **Average Game Length**: 30-50 moves
- **Key Features**: Seed sowing, capture rules

## Card Games 🃏

Games using standard or custom card decks.

### Poker (Texas Hold'em) 💰

- **Complexity**: High
- **Strategy Depth**: Very High
- **Players**: 2-6
- **Key Features**: Betting, bluffing, hand evaluation
- **Variants**: Cash game, tournament

### Texas Hold'em (Enhanced) 🎰

- **Complexity**: High
- **Strategy Depth**: Very High
- **Players**: 2-6
- **Key Features**: Advanced betting, side pots, rich UI
- **Special**: Full tournament support

### Dominoes 🁯

- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Strategy Depth**: Medium
- **Players**: 2-4
- **Key Features**: Tile matching, blocking strategy
- **Variants**: Block, draw, Mexican train

## Strategy Games 🗺️

Games focusing on strategic planning and resource management.

### Risk 🌍

- **Complexity**: High
- **Strategy Depth**: High
- **Players**: 2-6
- **Game Length**: 50-200 turns
- **Key Features**: Territory control, dice combat, reinforcements

### Monopoly 🏠

- **Complexity**: Medium-High
- **Strategy Depth**: Medium
- **Players**: 2-4
- **Game Length**: 50-150 turns
- **Key Features**: Property trading, development, auctions

### Battleship 🚢

- **Complexity**: Low-Medium
- **Strategy Depth**: Medium
- **Players**: 2
- **Key Features**: Hidden information, deduction
- **AI Features**: Smart targeting algorithms

## Social Deduction Games 🕵️

Games where players have hidden roles and must deduce information.

### Mafia 🌙

- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Strategy Depth**: High
- **Players**: 5-12
- **Key Features**: Day/night phases, role powers, voting
- **Roles**: Mafia, Detective, Doctor, Villager

### Among Us 🚀

- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Strategy Depth**: Medium-High
- **Players**: 4-10
- **Key Features**: Tasks, emergency meetings, impostor mechanics
- **Special**: Sabotage system

### Clue (Cluedo) 🔍

- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Strategy Depth**: Medium
- **Players**: 3-6
- **Key Features**: Deduction, note-taking, suggestions
- **Mystery Elements**: Suspect, weapon, location

## Abstract Strategy Games 🎯

Pure strategy games with perfect information.

### Connect 4 🔴🟡

- **Complexity**: Low
- **Strategy Depth**: Medium
- **Players**: 2
- **Game Length**: 20-40 moves
- **Key Features**: Gravity, 4-in-a-row detection

### Tic-Tac-Toe ❌⭕

- **Complexity**: Very Low
- **Strategy Depth**: Low
- **Players**: 2
- **Game Length**: 5-9 moves
- **Key Features**: Perfect play analysis

### Fox and Geese 🦊🦢

- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Strategy Depth**: Medium
- **Players**: 2 (asymmetric)
- **Key Features**: Different objectives per side
- **Special**: Asymmetric gameplay

### Nim 🥢

- **Complexity**: Low-Medium
- **Strategy Depth**: High (mathematical)
- **Players**: 2
- **Key Features**: Mathematical strategy, multiple variants

## Puzzle/Deduction Games 🧩

Games focused on logical deduction and puzzle-solving.

### Mastermind 🎨

- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Strategy Depth**: High
- **Players**: 2 (Codemaker vs Codebreaker)
- **Game Length**: 8-12 guesses
- **Key Features**: Pattern deduction, feedback system

## Other Games 🎭

Games that don't fit neatly into other categories.

### Debate 💬

- **Complexity**: High
- **Strategy Depth**: Very High
- **Players**: 2+ (with optional judge)
- **Key Features**: Structured arguments, rebuttals, scoring
- **Topics**: Customizable debate topics
